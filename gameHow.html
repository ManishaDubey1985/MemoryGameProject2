Memory game instructions:

It is easy to play a game and requires observation, concentration, and good memory to win.

  1. 1-2 players can play the game.
  2. The player must click on the grid and memorize it to match it with another picture.
  3. Once matched, the matching grids will freeze and be outlined in a square ring.
  4. When all the pictures match, the player will receive a winning message with the information of how many moves it took to win.
