How to play general Knowledge game??:

It is easy to play this game and test your IQ level.

  1. One player can play at a time and can test their IQ level.
  2. The user needs to select the option and then click the evaluate button to know the result.
  3. The next and previous buttons allows users to shuffle between questions.
  4. When the test is completed, clicking on the finish button will reset the test and will take you back to the first question.
